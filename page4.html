Best love songs

Stephen sanchez(Until i found her)


The song talks about how a certain guy swear to never fall in love again after he was heartbroken but all this changed when he or she met the one that brought butterflies to his or her stomach



Stephen sanchez captures love,feelings of heavenly nature being brought back to life just when you had thrown in the towel booom love comes in..


Ashley kutcher(from a distance)


Ashley talks of a certain past,memories intwertwined in her mind,she still dreams of his romeo .but sadly she can only love him from a distance.



The vocals and the feelings that this song pours to the ears just magical.Brings back a lot of memories.


Ruth B(Field of Dandelion)


Ruth B AKA the dandelion queen brings to his world of fantasy,she claims to see forever an everlasting love in his loved one.He claims that their is no other better than him,makes you wonder how does this thing called love work.



Talk of emotions ,love being poured out ooh my goodness,personally I Ondara this song has been one of my favourites for almost three years.


Billie Eilish(Ocean Eyes)


Ocean eyes sung by my favourite artist in the world is a song to make you cry,listen to the vocals amazing..



I love how she relates love with eyes,reason being sometimes our eyes say display and say it all especially what we fear saying out loud like I love you.


Lord Huron(Night we met)


This song simply talks of young love how it's always the best,many might disagree and say it's good to focus on your career the question is why so stressed about a live that can end any time?



The answer is always take me back to when i was young,no responsibilities just enjoying love and life.


Not You(Alan Walker)


Talks of a girl that tries to evade falling in love but then gets to meet a stranger whom she falls in love with but the question is,,would you rather date someone you love or someone who loves you?



The vocals of this song are just soo magical and unbeliavable.


Bruno Mars Ft Lady Gaga(Die with a smile)


Talks of being deep in love,and your only wish is to die with your beloved one,and by that you wont have to cry while dying but you shall be smiling as you slowly fade away.



Combination of the best vocals to bring love to live soo magical..


Adele(Fire to the rain)


Adele expresses her deepest feeling as she tries to explain how she felt as she lost he beloved one, soo deep that it may bring tears to your eyes.


Billie Eilish(I don't wanna be you)


She deeply talks of the tears that could be lost if she had to endure the pain of loosing her loved one..Talks of models who cry day in day out that their tears could fill swimming pools ,,how poetic



What a lovely song to listen to,,,,,


Avicii(Without)


Just listen to this lovely master piece the great legend Avicii(RIP) left us with,,Talks of the toughness of loosing someone you deeply had a connection with then poof,one day it all becomes a Fugazi,fairly dust never to be had from again.


Ed sheeran(Thinking out loud)


Last but not least Ed sheeran's Thinking out loud;Talks of how low is soo mysterios and how people fall in love in mysterious ways.
